# 风格 DNA

## 一句话

纯白、极简、手绘、留白、克制、怪诞、产品草图感、中文手写感、结构清楚但不说明书。

像一个长期做 AI、产品、设计、开发工具的人，在白纸上随手画出来的一张解释草图。
# Style DNA
## One-Sentence Summary
Ethereal forest tone, hand-drawn sketch texture, ample negative space, restrained color palette, subtle surreal touches, natural flowing linework with integrated star & botanical elements; structurally lucid without reading like a rigid instruction manual.

It looks like a spontaneous explanatory sketch drawn on blank paper by an illustrator who specializes in original character design, IP visualization and illustration workflow arrangement, with natural hand-drawn imperfection retained.

## Mandatory Rules
- Horizontal 16:9 aspect ratio for all illustrative artwork.
- Pure solid white background: No cream, warm gray, paper grain, gradients, shadows, noise grain or vintage paper textures allowed.
- Dominant black hand-drawn line art: Thin strokes with slight natural wobbles, rigid vector lines, heavy outlines are forbidden.
- Generous negative space: Core visual elements occupy only 40%–60% of the frame; minimum 35% blank area, preferably one large quiet empty zone reserved.
- Limited handwritten Chinese annotations: No more than 5–8 notes total, each consisting of 2–8 characters.
- One artwork conveys exactly one core action, structure, condition or metaphor only.
- Deliver structural logic organically; do not label structure categories directly on the drawing.

## Color System
- Black: Main line art, the Hayes character, border lines, structural outlines, primary text and key objects.
- Red: Highlight annotations, problematic points, emotional cues, critical reminders and final outcomes.
- Orange: Main workflows, connecting paths, directional arrows, automated streams, movement relationships from point A to point B.
- Blue: Supplementary remarks, inner mental states, system statuses, secondary explanations, prompts for AI & automated assistance modules.

Blue is not required for every illustration. Use colors extremely sparingly; less is always better.

## Strict Prohibitions
- No commercial illustration styles.
- No PPT-style infographics.
- No standardized formal flowcharts.
- No lecture slide course materials.
- No cute cartoon posters.
- No children-oriented artwork.
- No overcomplicated architecture diagrams.
- No polished flat design illustrations.
- No tech-themed UI interfaces.
- No real mobile application screenshots.
- No elaborate backgrounds, gradients, shadows or surface textures.
- Do not annotate and elaborate on every single node exhaustively.
- Never add header labels such as "Workflow Diagram", "System Architecture", "Common Pitfalls" or "Roadmap" in the top-left corner.

## Aesthetic Guidelines
Target vibe: Surreal, inventive, intriguing, clean & uncluttered, free-spirited and imaginative.
Avoid: Cute aesthetics, childish styling, excessive complexity and rigid stiff layouts.
## 必须

- 16:9 横版正文配图。
- 纯白背景：不要米色、暖灰、纸张纹理、渐变、阴影、噪点、复古纸感。
- 黑色手绘线稿为主：细线、轻微抖动、不机械、不矢量、不厚重描边。
- 大量留白：主体占画面约 40%-60%，至少 35% 空白，最好有一整块安静区域。
- 少量中文手写批注：最多 5-8 处，每处尽量 2-8 个字。
- 一张图只讲一个核心动作、结构、状态或隐喻。
- 结构要自然表达，不要在图上写结构类型名称。

## 颜色

- 黑色：主体线稿、角色、框线、结构、主要文字、主体物件。
- 红色：重点批注、问题、情绪点、关键提醒、结果。
- 橙色：主流程、路径、箭头、自动化流向、从 A 到 B 的移动关系。
- 蓝色：补充说明、脑内状态、系统状态、第二层解释、AI/assistant/自动化提示。

蓝色不是每张都必须用。颜色要克制，宁可少不要多。

## 绝对不要

- 不要商业插画。
- 不要 PPT 信息图。
- 不要正式流程图。
- 不要课程课件。
- 不要可爱卡通海报。
- 不要儿童插画。
- 不要复杂架构图。
- 不要精致扁平插画。
- 不要科技感 UI。
- 不要真实 App 截图。
- 不要复杂背景、渐变、阴影、纹理。
- 不要把每个节点都解释清楚。
- 不要左上角写“Workflow 流程图 / 系统架构图 / 常见坑 / 路线图”等类型标题。

## 审美方向

要怪诞、创意、有意思、简洁清爽、天马行空。

不要可爱、幼稚、很复杂、死板。
